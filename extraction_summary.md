Multiple vinegar varieties support the "21 Tweaks" weight-loss protocol:

### 3. Japanese Influence
Several items reflect Japanese umami-building techniques:
- Kombu in vegetable broth
- Miso paste combinations

### 4. Mediterranean Flavors
- Balsamic vinegar for salads

- **Umami Sauce:** Refrigerate up to 1 week or freeze in cubes
- **Vinegars:** Shelf-stable
- **Kombu:** Dried, rehydrates in cooking liquid
- **Tomato Puree:** Use salt-free versions only
